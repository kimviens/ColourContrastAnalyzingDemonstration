Text Contrast Basics

Explanation:

To pass the minimum requirements for text contrast:

  • regular sized text should have a contrast of 4.5:1 against its background
  • large scale text (at least 18pt / 24px or if bold 14pt / 18.5px)

Exception:

  • decorative text

Example 1: Normal sized text - Black text on blue background

Quisque viverra scelerisque nibh sed finibus. Donec pellentesque eget ligula eget finibus. Donec eget molestie erat, vel lacinia justo. Curabitur vulputate rutrum sapien, non tincidunt risus aliquam cursus.

Example 2: Normal sized text - White text on blue background

Mauris eu finibus enim, quis facilisis nibh. Cras in posuere risus. Nunc condimentum et metus a elementum. Sed at sagittis risus. Aliquam blandit volutpat fringilla.

Example 3: Normal sized text - Blue text on red background

Morbi malesuada viverra ligula, ut placerat mi auctor in. In sed egestas purus. Nullam in egestas mi. Proin sed mauris arcu. Nullam feugiat tellus efficitur, consequat leo sit amet, vulputate sapien. Praesent tincidunt sollicitudin dolor in dignissim.

Example 4: Large sized text - Purple text on green background

Praesent mollis ligula consectetur, vestibulum justo nec, vulputate purus. Suspendisse sed fringilla mauris. Morbi vehicula ante eu ornare consectetur.

Example 5: Large sized text - Blue text on pink background

Integer sodales ultrices massa sit amet tempus. Sed porta molestie ante, luctus ultricies eros vehicula at.

Example 6: Large sized text

Aenean sem augue, luctus a elementum tincidunt, vestibulum eget tellus. Etiam finibus id massa ut dignissim.

Example 7: Trick question

In gravida, odio at interdum gravida, mauris augue efficitur sapien, vel sollicitudin lectus elit quis massa. Vivamus tempor, est a ornare fringilla, magna sapien scelerisque ligula.

Non-Text Contrast Basics

Explanation:

To pass the minimum requirements for non-text contrast:

  • all parts necessary to understand the user interface component or graphical object must have a contrast ratio of at least 3:1 against adjacent colours

Exception:

  • decorative non-textual elements
